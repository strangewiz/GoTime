# Privacy Policy for GoTime

**Last updated:** February 13, 2026

The privacy policy is simple: no personal data shared with us will be given to any third party, under any circumstances. Your data will also never be used by us for any purpose without specific permission.

The app engages in no ad targeting, data mining, or other activities that may compromise your privacy, and we do not affiliate ourselves with any third parties that do so.

**Data Storage:**
*   **Local Storage**: All health data (voiding and bowel movement logs) is stored locally on your Apple Watch.
*   **iCloud (CloudKit)**: If enabled, data syncs to your personal private iCloud database. We (the developers) do not have access to this data.
*   **Apple Health**: If enabled, data syncs to the Apple Health app on your iPhone. This data is encrypted and managed by iOS.

**Contact Us:**
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our support page.
